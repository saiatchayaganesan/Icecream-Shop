Strawberry Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups strawberries, mashed
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

How It's Made:

Mash strawberries with sugar and let sit. Mix with cream, milk, and vanilla. Pour into an ice cream maker and churn.
