Chocolate Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

How It's Made:

Mix sugar and cocoa powder with cream and milk. Heat until warm, then chill. Pour into an ice cream maker and churn until thick.
